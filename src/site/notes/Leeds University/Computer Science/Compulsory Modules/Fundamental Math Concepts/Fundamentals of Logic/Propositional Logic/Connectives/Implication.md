###### Converse
- The converse of $p \rightarrow q$ is $q \rightarrow p$
###### Contrapositive
- The contrapositive of $p \rightarrow q$ is $\neg p \rightarrow \neg p$ 
Can be shown as $( \neg p \lor q )$
