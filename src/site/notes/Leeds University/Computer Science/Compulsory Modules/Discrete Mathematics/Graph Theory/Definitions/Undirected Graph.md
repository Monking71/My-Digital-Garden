*Multigraphs* allow *loops* (vertex to itself) and *parallel edges* (multiple edges with same connections)
```mermaid
graph LR
v1---v1
v1---v2
v1---v3
v2---v3
v2---v4
v4---v4
v4---v5
v4---v5
```
