- Left most bit is the ***sign bit***
- 0 means positive
- 1 means negative
- $+18\ =\ 0001\ 0010$
- $-18\ =\ 1001\ 0010$
#### Problems
- We need to consider both sign and magnitude in arithmetic
- Two representations of zero $(+\ 0\ and\ -0)$
